**FINDING:** Enterprise software companies that successfully built brand while protecting IP typically abstracted their proprietary layers by several levels: they published the *business outcome narrative* (what customers experience), documented the *methodology principles* (how the approach thinks about problems), and released *partial technical implementation* (enough to demonstrate sophistication without revealing the core algorithm or data architecture).
